Planning mockups v3

Keep the camp warmth. Bring back the product strengths.

This pass keeps the flatter, warmer direction but folds in what the old site did right: the full visual keyboard, mobile key behavior, lesson mastery at a glance, and small "start typing" cues that make the experience feel responsive instead of static.

Typography direction: rounded UI text for warmth, highly legible test text for accuracy, and compact labels that support recognition instead of making users remember where they left off.

Desktop defaults to the full keyboard.
Mobile defaults to compact phone keys.
Lesson stars show mastery in the menu.
Games reuse the same typing engine.
Stats stay post-test or signed-in.
Camping accents support, not dominate.
