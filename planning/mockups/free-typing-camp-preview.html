Mockup v3 recovery

Modernize the site without throwing away the good product ideas.

This version explicitly carries forward the old experience where it worked: full keyboard on desktop, compact mobile keys on phones, lesson stars in the menu, labeled lesson drills, calculator-style games, and clear ready states before the first keystroke.

The visual direction stays closer to your preferred flat education product references: warm paper, deep camp-night navigation, campfire orange actions, muted pine accents, and illustration used as a supporting detail rather than a wallpaper.

Default desktop: full keyboard with next-key highlight.
Default mobile: compact phone keys with optional full layout.
Lessons: visible level labels, stars, next drill, and progress.
Games: small typing drills using shared input and result logic.
Stats: shown after tests or in the signed-in dashboard.
Typography: friendly UI, highly legible typing text.
